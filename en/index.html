About Me

Deepali bhosale sayed is well known film actress. She has worked in many hindi, marathi movies, serials, advertisement and plays. She has huge fan following. Being celebrity she understands her responsibilities and social welfare.

Deepali bhosale sayed is currently Mahila President of Shivsangram maharashtra state, Chairperson of Dadasaheb Falke MSK Trust, President of cineabhinetri Deepali bhosale sayed Foundation, Brand ambassador of suwarnsparsh Jewellery, Simply EAT products.

Trusts

DADASAHEB PHALKE MSK TRUST

Dadasaheb Phalke Marathi Sanskrutik Kary Trust is a non-profit Charitable Trust and our aim is to Education and Healthcare, Housing and Rehabilitation, Employment and Empowerment, Entertainment and Environment Sustainability, Water and Sanitation.
DEEPALI BHOSALE SAYED CHARITABLE TRUST

This is newly founded non-profit trust and our aim is to build a full-fledged Old Age Orphanage Home (Vridhaashram) in Gundegaon, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra.

Social Service

EYE CHECK UP CAMP

We had conducted eye check up camp on 10th January 2015 at Bordi, Palghar Maharashtra. In this occasion we had distribute so many optical to the peoples those who are done the eye check up with our camp. The medical advisers and technical experts are coordinated the program and many people has taken the benefit of the same.

SCHOOL BAG DISTRIBUTION

We had conducted a program of School bag distribution on 11th January 2015 at Bordi, Palghar Maharashtra. In this occasion we had distributed so many school bags to the needy students those who are surviving in financial deficiency and they are coming from the Adivasi community.

CLOTH DISTRIBUTION

We had conducted a program of cloth distribution camp on 11th January 2015 at Bordi, Palghar, Maharashtra. In this occasion we have distributed cloths to the Adivasi community near by the Bordi locality. Lot of peoples are come and collect the cloths from our distribution camp.

SOCIAL WELFARE PROGRAM

We had conducted a social welfare program at Mungeli Village, Chhattisgarh on 8th January 2015. Many peoples in the Adivasi community and other community are attended in this program and they were acknowledge and understood about their health issues. Our medical experts and other knowledgeable personalities had explained the importance of health and how to maintain the health in good condition. We had distributed some medicine in Adivasi community and many people get the facility of free medicine.
